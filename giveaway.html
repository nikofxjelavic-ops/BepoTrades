Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the giveaway.

How do I enter the giveaways?
Buy an evaluation from any partnered prop firm using code BEPO at checkout. Then connect your Discord above and submit your order number using the form on this page.
How many entries can I have?
There is no cap. One evaluation equals one entry, so the more evals you buy with code BEPO, the more entries you stack and the better your chances of winning.
When are winners picked?
Every single week. Winners are drawn every Sunday from all eligible entries submitted during that week (Sunday 12:00 AM – Saturday 11:59 PM ET).
How do I know if I won?
Winners are announced in the #announcements channel in the BepoTrades Discord server every Sunday. Make sure your Discord notifications are on so you don't miss it.
Is there a free way to enter?
Yes. No purchase is necessary. A free entry option is available with the same odds as purchase entries. Open a support ticket in the BepoTrades Discord for details.
